隐私政策

拍立记 / PixNote · English ↓

最后更新日期：2026 年 7 月

1. 信息收集

拍立记是一个注重隐私的应用。我们收集的信息仅限于您主动提供的内容：

  • 您拍摄或从相册选择的照片
  • 您在照片背面书写的文字（标题、正文）
  • 照片的拍摄日期和地点（从照片 EXIF 元数据中自动提取，或由您手动输入）

这些数据仅存储在您的设备上，并通过您的 iCloud 账户在您的 Apple 设备之间同步。

2. 数据使用

您的数据仅用于以下目的：

  • 在 App 内展示您的照片和背面文字
  • 通过 iCloud 在您的设备间同步
  • 生成分享图片供您分享到其他平台
  • 提供「那年今日」桌面小组件和通知提醒

我们不会将您的数据上传到任何第三方服务器，不会用于广告投放，不会进行用户画像分析。

3. 数据存储与同步

iCloud 同步由 Apple 提供，遵循 Apple 的隐私保护标准。您的照片和文字通过端到端加密存储在 iCloud 中，只有您的 Apple ID 可以访问。您可以在「设置 > iCloud > 管理存储空间」中随时管理或删除同步数据。

4. 数据分享

您可以选择以下方式主动分享照片：

  • 合成长图分享：生成包含照片和文字的长图，分享到社交媒体
  • 近距离分享（AirDrop）：通过 Apple AirDrop 传输照片及背面内容
  • 加密分享：设置密码生成加密照片，接收方需在拍立记 App 中输入正确密码才能查看

所有分享行为均由您主动发起，拍立记不会自动分享任何数据。

5. 相机与相册权限

拍立记仅在您主动拍照或选择照片时访问相机或相册。拍摄的照片仅保存在 App 内和您的 iCloud 中。

6. 位置信息

我们仅在您拍照时获取当前位置（用于记录拍摄地点），或在您从相册导入照片时从照片 EXIF 元数据中读取位置。位置数据仅存储在您的设备上，用于在地图上展示拍摄足迹。您可以随时编辑或删除位置信息。

7. 订阅与内购

付费会员通过 Apple App Store 进行，支付信息由 Apple 处理，我们无法访问您的支付详情。

8. 儿童隐私

本应用不针对 13 岁以下儿童设计。我们不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。

9. 联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请通过以下方式联系我们：
邮箱：app.pixnote@icloud.com

10. 政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策将在 App 内公布。

Privacy Policy

中文 ↑

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information Collection

PixNote is a privacy-focused application. The only information we collect is what you actively provide:

  • Photos you take or select from your photo library
  • Text you write on the back of photos (title, body)
  • Photo date and location (auto-extracted from EXIF metadata, or manually entered by you)

This data is stored solely on your device and synced across your Apple devices through your iCloud account.

2. Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Displaying your photos and back-side text within the app
  • Syncing across your devices via iCloud
  • Generating share images for you to share to other platforms
  • Providing the "On This Day" widget and notification reminders

We do not upload your data to any third-party servers, do not use it for advertising, and do not build user profiles.

3. Data Storage & Sync

iCloud sync is provided by Apple and follows Apple's privacy standards. Your photos and text are stored in iCloud with end-to-end encryption, accessible only with your Apple ID. You can manage or delete synced data anytime in Settings > iCloud > Manage Storage.

4. Data Sharing

You may choose to share photos through:

  • Composite Image: Generate a long image combining front and back, ready for social media
  • AirDrop: Transfer the photo and its back content via Apple AirDrop
  • Encrypted Share: Create a password-protected encrypted photo. The recipient must enter the correct password in PixNote to view the back content

All sharing is initiated by you. PixNote never shares data automatically.

5. Camera & Photo Library Permissions

PixNote accesses your camera or photo library only when you actively take a photo or select one. Photos taken are saved only within the app and your iCloud.

6. Location Information

We only access your current location when you take a photo (to record where it was taken), or read location from EXIF metadata when you import from your photo library. Location data is stored only on your device. You can edit or delete location information at any time.

7. Subscriptions & In-App Purchases

Paid features are processed through the Apple App Store. Payment information is handled by Apple; we cannot access your payment details.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact:
Email: app.pixnote@icloud.com

10. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updated policies will be published within the app.
